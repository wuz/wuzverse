# Clockwork Atlas

> This post is a work-in-progress of a Fabula Ultima atlas I'm working on in my
> spare time. At some point, this content will likely be collected into a zine
> or book, but until then, I'm gonna post it here!

- [Artiste](/posts/ca-artiste)

I have a couple goals with this class:

1. **To add some kind of true "summoning" to Fabula Ultima.** The Arcanum and
   Spiritist classes are summoning-adjacent, but I want this class to feel like
   a battlefield commander controlling its summons.
2. **No bogging down combat with tons of turns/actions for your summons.** This
   class should feel as quick and easy to play as any other class.
3. **Two possible "tracks" for this class.** You can either go for lots of
   summons that do their own thing _or_ use your summons to bolster yourself and
   allies.

# Artiste

## ALSO: Creator, Imbuer, Drafter

An _Artiste_ has learned to divide a small piece of their soul into everything
they create.
Some Artistes use paint, others use the written word, and still others create
their art in a unique way.

Users of **Création** magic find inspiration in the world around them, allowing
them to bring their art to life to aid them in and out of battle.

- Who taught you the art of Création?
- You create beautiful, temporary things.
  How does that impact you?
- What kind of art do you create?
  How does that reflect your inner view of the world?
- What do your Créations look like?

## Artiste Free Benefits

- Permanently increase your maximum Mind Points by 5.
- You may perform Rituals whose effects fall within the **Ritualism** disipline

# Artiste

## ALSO: Creator, Imbuer, Drafter

An _Artiste_ has learned to divide a small piece of their soul into everything

Some Artistes use paint, others use the written word, and still others create

Users of **Création** magic find inspiration in the world around them, allowing

- Who taught you the art of Création?
- You create beautiful, temporary things.
  How does that impact you?
- What kind of art do you create?
  How does that reflect your inner view of the world?
- What do your Créations look like?

## Artiste Free Benefits

- Permanently increase your maximum Mind Points by 5.
- You may perform Rituals whose effects fall within the **Ritualism** disipline

## Artiste Skills

### Agité ✦ 5

Damage done by your Créations is increased by ⦗SL⦘.

### Création Magic ✦ 10

Each time you acquire this Skill, learn one Création spell.
Offensive (⚡) Creation spells use ⦗ WLP + DEX ⦘ for the Magic Check.

### Weapon of Création

You may equip your preferred artist tools in your main hand slot.
As long as you have your tools equipped, you may treat them as the following
melee **Arcane** weapon:

:::callout

#### Créarme

**Accuracy** ⦗WLP + DEX⦘

**Damage** ⦗HR + 5⦘ Deals extra damage equal to your ⦗SL⦘ in **Agité** (above)

:::

### Blank Canvas

You may use an action to absorb the magic powering your Créations.
For each Création absorbed in this way, immediately recover 5 **MP** and destroy
the Création.

### Ritual Création

You may perform Rituals whose effects fall within the **Création** discipline.
Création Rituals use ⦗ WLP + DEX ⦘ for the Magic Check.

## Création Spells

### Exquisite Corpse

**MP** 15 | **Target** Three creatures | **Duration** Instantaneous

Choose 3 targets.
Each target performs a free attack.
If the attack misses, the target takes the damage instead.

### Chroma Pulse ⚡

**MP** 5 | **Target** One creatures | **Duration** Instantaneous

One of your Créations releases its power in an explosive pulse.
The target suffers ⦗ HR + 15 ⦘ light damage and one drafted Création is
destroyed.

### Oeuvre

**MP** 5 x T | **Target** Up to X targets | **Duration** Instantaneous Target as
many creatures as you have Créations.
Detonate all your Créations.
Each target suffers ⦗ HR + 15 ⦘ light damage and all of your Créations are
destroyed.

### Avant-garde

**MP** 5 | **Target** One creature | **Duration** Scene

Three of your drafted Créations merge together, creating a large shield.
The target is considered covered (as if by the Guard action).
This spell ends at the start of your next turn.

### Chiaroscuro

**MP** 10 x T | **Target** Up to three creatures | **Duration** Instantaneous

Draft a barrage short-lived, offensive Créations at your targets.
Each target suffers ⦗ HR + 10 ⦘ split equally between light and dark damage.

### Draft Création

**MP** 5 | **Target** Self | **Duration** Scene

You draft a Création to the field.

:::callout

#### Création

**HP:** 3

**Damage:** 1

_The power of Création made physical._

On your turn, you can use an action to attack with all the Créations you have
summoned.
Treat this attack as if you were attacking with a weapon with the **multi (X)**
property, where X is the number of Créations you have summoned.

:::

### Font of Inspiration ⚡

**MP** 5 | **Target** One creature | **Duration** Instantaneous Destroy one of
your creations to remove all status effects from the target.

### Brilliant Strike ⚡

**MP** 20 | **Target** One Creature | **Duration** Instantaneous

You draft a short-lived, offensive Création towards your foe.
The target suffers ⦗ HR + 25 ⦘ light damage.
**Opportunity:** A target hit by this spell suffers dazed.

### Imbue

**MP** 10 | **Target** One Weapon | **Duration** Scene

You draft the power of Création into a weapon.
Until the spell ends, when this weapon deals damage, one of your Creations can
make a free attack against the same target.

This spell can only be cast on a weapon equipped by a willing creature.
